About Quickmart Kenya

Quickmart is Kenya's leading retail supermarket chain, committed to providing quality products at affordable prices to communities across the country.

Founded with the vision of making shopping convenient and accessible, we have grown to serve thousands of customers daily through our expanding network of branches.

Our mission is to deliver exceptional customer service, maintain the highest standards of product quality, and create employment opportunities for Kenyans.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We conduct business with honesty and transparency
  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in everything we do
  • Customer Focus: Our customers are at the heart of our business
  • Innovation: We embrace new ideas and continuous improvement
  • Community: We give back to the communities we serve

Terms & Conditions

Last Updated: November 2025

Application Terms

By submitting a job application to Quickmart Kenya, you agree to the following terms and conditions:

1. Application Processing Fee

A non-refundable processing fee of KES 150 is required to complete your application. This fee covers administrative costs associated with processing, verification, and review of applications.

2. Information Accuracy

You confirm that all information provided in your application is accurate and truthful. Providing false information may result in disqualification or termination if employed.

3. Background Verification

Quickmart Kenya reserves the right to conduct background checks and verify all information provided in your application.

4. Application Review

Applications will be reviewed within 14 business days. Only shortlisted candidates will be contacted for interviews.

5. No Guarantee of Employment

Submission of an application does not guarantee employment. All hiring decisions are at the sole discretion of Quickmart Kenya.

6. Data Usage

By applying, you consent to the collection, storage, and processing of your personal information as outlined in our Privacy Policy.

Cookie Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and improving site functionality.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your application progress
  • Keep you logged in during your session
  • Analyze site traffic and usage patterns
  • Improve website performance and user experience

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and choices
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the application portal.
