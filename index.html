About Quickmart Kenya

Quickmart is one of Kenya's fastest-growing supermarket chains, committed to providing quality products and exceptional customer service across the nation.

Our Mission

To be Kenya's preferred retail destination by offering quality products, excellent customer service, and competitive prices while creating employment opportunities for Kenyans.

Our Vision

To become the leading supermarket chain in East Africa, known for innovation, customer satisfaction, and community impact.

Cookie Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide a better user experience and understand how you interact with our site.

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic features like page navigation and access to secure areas.

2. Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website, such as which pages are visited most often. This helps us improve our website performance.

3. Functionality Cookies

These cookies allow our website to remember choices you make (such as your preferred language) and provide enhanced features.

4. Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how users interact with our site, helping us improve user experience and content.

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • View what cookies are stored and delete them individually
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block all cookies
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services (like Google Analytics) that also set cookies. These services help us analyze website traffic and improve our services.

Your Consent

By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies in accordance with this policy. You can withdraw consent by adjusting your browser settings.

Updates to This Policy
